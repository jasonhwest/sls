Since life changes and challenges often impact multiple areas of life and others around us, {Simplified} offers counseling and coaching to assist in all areas affected by these changes. Whether it is an exciting life event such as a new baby or an engagement or an unfortunate situation such as a divorce or family member in an addiction, {Simplified} Life Solutions offers a team of helping professionals to empower, encourage and equip you during your particular journey.

Counseling:

  • Individual: Tweens. Teens. Young Professionals and Adults.
  • Marriage: Pre engagement. Engagement. Marriage. Divorce Recovery.
  • Family: Parents and Children/Teens.

Mom Coaching:

Many new moms report feeling overwhelmed with their new baby and unsure of how they are going to make it through the next few months, much less the next few days! Mom Coaching offers phone consultations for new moms on important parenting issues such as sleep patterns, feeding, attachment, and self care.

Addictions Coaching:

Our addictions coaching services offer individuals and family support services through a 5 session intensive method. If additional services are needed after the initial intensive, the {Simplified} professional will continue in consulting or refer to qualified addiction inpatient or outpatient service as necessary.
