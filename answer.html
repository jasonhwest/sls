Counseling and Coaching Fees

Session fees are for a 50-55 min session and include brief communications between sessions as needed. For more information on fees for counseling services, feel free to email or call the professional specializing in your area of need.

Scheduling and Hours

You may schedule your counseling or coaching session easily through our online scheduling program.

Insurance

While insurance is not accepted for counseling services, invoices are offered for individuals to submit to their insurance providers. It is suggested that individuals contact their insurance providers regarding reimbursements since the percentages reimbursed often varies depending on the insurance provider and plan.

Payment Options

Credit card, debit cards, health savings cards, checks and cash are accepted for payments.

*Proceeds from sessions go towards local and international organizations committed to serve the psychosocial, emotional and physical needs of others. For more information, see the blog.
